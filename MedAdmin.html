Medication Administration

Here are a few tips for administration:

  • Make sure the barrel of the syringe reads “U-100”
  • Check for any lumps or crystals in the bottom
  • Roll the bottle gently between your hands about 20 times until the liquid has an even look
  • Pull the syringe slowly to avoid air bubbles
  • Pinch up the area of skin that you plan to inject
