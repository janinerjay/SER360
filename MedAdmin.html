Medication Administration

Here are a few tips for administration:

1. Make sure the barrel of the syringe reads “U-100”
2. Check for any lumps or crystals in the bottom
3. Roll the bottle gently between your hands about 20 times
until the liquid has an even look
4. Pull the syringe slowly to avoid air bubbles
5. Pinch up the area of skin that you plan to inject
